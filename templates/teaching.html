Presentation and Communication

A highlight of my mathematical journey has always been the opportunity to share my passion with others. I have had the privilege of presenting at seven notable conferences (a selection of which is listed below) and numerous seminars. My teaching experience is extensive; I've led several courses and served as a teaching assistant for more than ten classes throughout my academic career. Beyond formal teaching, I've honed my communication and presentation skills through Outreach programs, where I've mentored middle school and undergraduate students, fostering their interest and understanding in mathematics.

In the background, you can see me presenting my work on Social Distancing Dynamics at the 2022 AMS Fall Eastern Sectional Meeting, Special Session on Game-Theoretic and Agent-Based Approaches to Modeling Biological and Social Systems, University of Massachusetts Amherst.
